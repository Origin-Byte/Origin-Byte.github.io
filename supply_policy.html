    0x0::supply_policy

    Module contaning SupplyPolicy type.

    A SupplyPolicy can be regulated or unregulated. Regulated policies can have a ceiling on the maximum supply and keep track of the current supply, whilst unregulated policies have no supply constraints nor they keep track of the number of minted objects.
