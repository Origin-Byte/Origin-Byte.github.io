    supply

    0x0::supply

    Module contaning Supply type.

    Supply object are meant to control the supply of a given object type, or a given accumualtion of actions of a given type. It tracks the current supply and guarantees that it cannot surpass the maximum supply defined.

    Supply can be frozen, therefore making it impossible to change the maximum supply.
